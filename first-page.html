First Announcement

14th International HCH and Pesticides Forum

February 21-24, 2023, Zaragoza, Spain

Theme:

How the European Union is managing the Legacy of Lindane and HCH waste. What are the “Lessons learned” and what is the “Way forward”

The 14th Forum discusses the State of the art on POPs elimination 31 years after the 1st Forum in Zwolle, the Netherlands

Organizers:

  • Department of Agriculture, Livestock and Environment, Contaminated Soil Service, Government of Aragon, Spain.
  • International HCH & Pesticides Association (IHPA), The Netherlands.
  • SARGA, Aragonese Society of Agro-environmental Management, Government of Aragon, Spain.

Co-organizers:

  • Lindanet – European Network of Lindane waste affected regions working together towards a greener environment
  • EU LIFE Project: LIFEPOPSWAT
  • Free University of Berlin, Germany
